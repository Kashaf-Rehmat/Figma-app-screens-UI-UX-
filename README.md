# The Last Call

## Overview
The Last Call is an HCI-based platform designed to reduce food waste by connecting supermarkets and grocery stores with businesses that can purchase near-expiry products in bulk.

The platform helps sellers recover value from products that are approaching their expiry date while enabling buyers to purchase items at discounted prices.

## Problem Statement
Large quantities of food are wasted daily because supermarkets cannot sell products before their expiry dates. This results in:

- Food waste
- Financial losses
- Environmental impact

The Last Call provides a digital solution to connect these sellers with potential bulk buyers.
